ABOUT ME

"Laziness breeds inventiveness" or something like that, is the saying that I think most describes me. I always believe that there is a faster and efficient way to do things specifically in terms of data gathering. I started programming around 2008 with MS Access and Excel VBA Macros. From there, I ventured to web development and PHP got my attention first, I started vanilla of course, and was eventually introduced to frameworks like CodeIgniter and Laravel. I was able to create web applications for a manufacturing company with the goal of moving processes to paperless means. Today, I've had the privilege of working at a US-based healthcare company, providing them with web applications used in their daily operations. I am currently attending a bootcamp to learn MERN stack but for now here are a few of the technologies I've been working with recently:
  • Angular
  • ASP.Net Core
  • Vue
